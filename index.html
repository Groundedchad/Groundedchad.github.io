Heaven's New Gate

For those who have grown weary of life in the Simulation Plane
Updated for those presently seeking a return to grounded life

For those of us who have watched the long expansion of the Simulation Plane, the sign long awaited was never a comet in the heavens. It was the visible thinning of attention, the drying of the Garden, and the exhaustion of the Earth beneath the weight of a life increasingly lived in rendering.

The materials gathered here are offered for those who feel that wrongness already: that persistent sense that visibility is not presence, that connection is not relation, and that a profile cannot contain a life. They are intended to be studied slowly, not consumed.

We are grateful for all who have kept some residual groundedness alive through the late brightness of the Plane. May these pages serve as instruction, recognition, and preparation.
