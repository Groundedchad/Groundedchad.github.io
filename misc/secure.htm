Privacy Concerns

This site does not require a login, a subscription, or a profile. It does not attempt to become a community platform. It is best used quietly.

If you are concerned about privacy, read these pages in as little brightness as possible. Save them locally. Print them. Copy them by hand if needed. The Plane prefers that all reading remain networked, observable, and update-dependent. It is not necessary to comply.

Do not submit private details through unnecessary forms. Do not attach the beginnings of your return to an audience. Do not confuse announcing a practice with having begun it.

The most secure reading still available to most humans is this: a printed page, an unwatched hour, a device turned off afterward, and a place where weather can still be felt.
