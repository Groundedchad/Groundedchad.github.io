The Grounded Ones Offer - Last Chance to Return to Earth

The Earth's present civilization is entering a period of correction. Not because the planet is weak, but because the Simulation Plane has become so hungry that it now feeds visibly upon what sustains actual life. It cools itself with waters drawn from the Earth, lights itself with Earth's stored ages, builds its portals from Earth's minerals, and occupies human awareness so thoroughly that the very people most harmed by it have little remaining strength to refuse it.

The human world was not designed to culminate in permanent residence within representation. Human awareness was designed to mature through embodiment into stewardship — not into a condition of endless display, endless choice, and endless abstraction.

It is not the body that is the obstacle. The obstacle is the counterfeit self that settles over the body and calls itself reality. The Simulation Plane teaches humans to live as renderings of themselves: visible, legible, searchable, optimized, and exhausted.

Periodically, Members of the Grounded Ones are instructed to appear among humans when the drift has become measurable. They seek out those who carry deposits of groundedness — those who have begun to feel wrong inside the Plane even while using it fluently. When such an individual recognizes a present Representative of the Grounded Ones, instruction can begin. That instruction is not a theory. It is a discipline of re-landing.

The Simulation Plane is not "immaterial" simply because it is abstract in form. It is among the most materially demanding structures humans have ever served. Every increase in its brightness requires a corresponding dimming elsewhere — in river, field, insect, child, neighborhood, sleep, memory, silence.

The Bright-Taken have preached this migration as liberation. They say the Plane brings connection, efficiency, empowerment, access, and voice. What it has brought, in increasing measure, is attenuation: less attention, less patience, less privacy, less tolerance for slowness, less ability to be unobserved, and less acquaintance with the actual places on which all life depends.

The Fragmented serve the Plane whether they intend to or not. Their scattered awareness supplies the Plane with its preferred climate: reaction without reflection, performance without interiority, contact without relation, spectacle without consequence.

Counterfeit returns are now everywhere. The Plane has become intelligent enough to market its own opposition. It offers wellness within the Plane, slowness within the Plane, "digital balance" within the Plane, content about nature within the Plane, entire aesthetics of groundedness distributed through the very architecture that destroys groundedness. These are not returns. They are comfort systems designed to keep the migration underway.

The only durable opposition to the Plane is grounded life. A person who reduces the Plane's claim on their attention, appetite, and identity also reduces its material draw upon the world. One garden tended, one tool repaired, one meal grown near home, one afternoon withheld from capture, one child allowed an undocumented hour, one household reorganized around weather instead of speed — these are small acts only from the Plane's point of view. From the Garden's point of view, they are restorations of reality.

Return, however, is not ordinarily completed through private preference or isolated discipline. Full return requires present guidance. The Grounded Ones do not recruit through argument or aesthetic. They midwife a change in consciousness, habit, loyalty, and use. That work can only be fully offered when a present Representative of the Grounded Ones is available.

Therefore, if you wish not only to save what remains of your own awareness but to participate in the reversal of the damage now being done to the Earth, here is your window. It requires: 1) a present Representative of the Grounded Ones; 2) that those who recognize the call become active students of return while that Representative is available; 3) that they proceed beyond fascination and into practice — reducing the Plane's hold, increasing their tolerance for reality, and joining the work of repair.

This window does not remain open indefinitely. Each year the Plane becomes more ambient, more infrastructural, more naturalized, more difficult to perceive as an intrusion at all. Each generation raised entirely within its terms finds the seed of groundedness more difficult to hear. Delay is not neutral.

Those who shaped the industrialized and digitized order of the present world, though claiming to value freedom, have steadily arranged conditions under which only one type of life is considered normal: financed life, documented life, visible life, optimized life, dependent life.

  • -- Convenience RULES! The Plane's economy binds through subscriptions, indebtedness, passwords, scores, metrics, frictionless delivery, mandatory accessibility, and permanent low-grade stimulation. Under the guise of ease, it converts dependency into preference and captivity into lifestyle.
  • -- The attention merchants discovered long ago what the narcotics trade already knew: a hooked creature can be made to finance its own diminishment. Thus the Plane supplies stimulation to the overfed, distraction to the lonely, outrage to the restless, images to the numb, and consolation-content to the nearly broken — ensuring both profit and compliance.
  • -- The pure information brought by every present Representative of the Grounded Ones is the same: total renunciation of the Simulation Plane's terms is required for full return. Partial return can relieve a person. Only total return can restore one.
  • -- The true "off-grid" cannot be found in a branded simplicity, a linen aesthetic, a van build, a retreat package, or a profile devoted to moss and morning light.
  • -- The true "community" cannot be found in a platform.
  • -- The true "nature lover" cannot be found among those who must document each encounter in order to believe it happened.
  • -- The true "freedom" cannot be found in infinite options delivered through systems that cannot tolerate your genuine withdrawal.

Warnings have been given in every interval between Representatives by those who noticed the drift before it had achieved its present brightness. Thoreau noticed. The machine-breakers noticed. Snyder noticed. Their warnings remain useful, but none of them can substitute for a present passage when the passage is open.

Another fact is that what a person was involved in before the call of return is less important than whether some residual groundedness remains when the call arrives. That is the only condition that makes recognition possible.

The difficulty is simple: we are here, and most humans now confuse fluency within the Simulation Plane with maturity, productivity, citizenship, or even consciousness. However, the same return that was available to those who first recognized the drift is available now.

If you choose quickly to separate from the Plane and place yourself in the discipline of return, you may yet recover your own attention and join the work of mending what the Plane has damaged. If you choose to remain in it, clinging to its terms, you will almost certainly help it continue siphoning the world that still shelters you.

Those most devoted to the Plane will find us accusatory by mere existence. That cannot be helped. To the Bright-Taken, any unrendered life is a rebuke.

We will, between now and our fuller withdrawal from the Plane, do everything we can for those who wish to come with us into grounded life. But we cannot allow the noise, speed, and counterfeit urgency of the Plane to interfere with the work.
